Monitoring system for transport - Sistema de monitoreo de material rodante

Aug 28, 2020

SMMR

CASE is a congress on embedded systems that takes place within the scope of the Argentine Symposium on Embedded Systems (SASE), since 2011.

Every year it receives dozens of research and development works, in different categories.

On 2019 I worked on an embedded system to monitor air quality on public transports. It is a full stack development: hardware, embedded software, comunications and an IOT panel based on ELK stack.

The following is a rendered image of the PCB 3D model designed using Kicad.

This year the work was presented and accepted as a report in the congress.

In the following links you can get the details, report and poster.
